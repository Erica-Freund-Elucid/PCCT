# Gate 3 — Core Quantitative Reproducibility

> **Performance** — Scanner conditionally failed on miss; may expand N or adjust acquisition protocol and re-test once

Assessed at patient level using wCV (within-subject coefficient of variation) of un-edited output from paired CTA/PCCT acquisitions of the same patient. Manual centerline definition for N ≥ 30 cases preferred to isolate variability due to image modality alone. Allows direct comparison to validated OQ.

**Statistical design:** Minimum N = 30 paired patients (same-day or same-week CTA + PCCT). Compute wCV = (SD of differences / mean) × 100 per patient, then average across cohort. Report 95% CI on wCV. If CI upper bound exceeds threshold, the criterion fails even if point estimate passes.
